The Fisherman's Wharf has been delighting the multi-cultural Goan community with the most authentic Goan cuisine since 2005. The original seafood multi-cuisine restaurant, located on the banks of the scenic River Sal in South Goa, has earned a reputation for excellence. Building on this success, the founder company has expanded the venture, taking the pride of Goa to other parts of India and beyond. The menu in homage to the treasures of the sea, is cooked with the unique zest of the Goan kitchen. We take pride in our authentic Goan dessert, the freshly made Bebinca, which is not to be missed. Come and experience the true taste of Goa at The Fisherman's Wharf, where every dish is a celebration of the region's rich culinary heritage.
